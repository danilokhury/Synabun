# Prompt Library — Curated Templates

Use these as foundations and adapt to the user's specific needs. All prompts follow Leonardo.ai best practices.

## Video Prompt Templates

### Cinematic / Film

**Epic Establishing Shot**
"Slow crane up over a vast mountain valley at dawn, morning mist pooling between dark pine ridges, the first golden light catching snow-capped peaks in the distance, a winding river reflects copper sky, the camera continues rising to reveal the endless landscape stretching to the horizon"

**Character Introduction**
"Dolly in toward a figure standing at the edge of a rain-soaked rooftop at night, city lights blurring into bokeh below, their coat collar turned up against the wind, neon signs reflecting in puddles at their feet, the camera pushes slowly to a medium close-up as they turn to face the lens"

**Dramatic Reveal**
"Super dolly in through a darkened corridor, light spilling from an opening ahead, dust particles floating in the beam, the camera accelerates as it reaches the threshold and bursts into a vast sunlit cathedral interior, stained glass casting prismatic light across stone columns"

### Product / Commercial

**Product Hero Shot**
"Smooth orbit left around a matte black wireless speaker floating on a clean gradient background, studio rim lighting creating crisp edge highlights, subtle reflection on the surface below, the camera completes a quarter rotation revealing the speaker grille texture and LED indicator"

**Lifestyle Integration**
"Dolly right following a hand placing a ceramic coffee mug on a sunlit wooden table, steam rising from the surface catching golden morning light through linen curtains, shallow depth of field with a blurred kitchen garden visible through the window"

### Nature / Landscape

**Golden Hour Landscape**
"Crane up over a field of wild lavender at sunset, wind creating rippling waves across the purple surface, a lone oak tree silhouetted against a gradient sky of amber and deep violet, fireflies beginning to emerge in the warm air, the camera rises to reveal rolling hills beyond"

**Dramatic Weather**
"Dolly in toward a lighthouse on a rocky cliff during a storm, massive waves crashing against dark basalt, spray exploding upward, the lighthouse beam cutting through sheets of rain, dark thunderclouds rolling overhead with distant lightning illuminating the churning sea"

### Abstract / Artistic

**Fluid Dynamics**
"Macro dolly in on luminous ink drops falling into clear water in slow motion, magenta and gold pigment expanding in organic tendrils, light refracting through the mixing colors creating iridescent patterns, the camera pushes through the microscopic landscape of swirling fluid"

**Geometric Transformation**
"Orbit right around a cluster of floating metallic polyhedra catching studio light, each surface reflecting a different color, the shapes slowly rotate and reconfigure into new arrangements, sharp geometric edges creating precise shadow patterns on a matte white background"

### Social Media / Short Form

**Trending Hook**
"Crash zoom in on a pair of hands unboxing a glossy product from minimalist packaging, tissue paper crinkling, the item emerges catching direct light, camera settles into a tight detail shot revealing the texture and craftsmanship, clean white background with soft shadows"

**Satisfying Loop**
"Top-down dolly in on a precise pour of thick caramel over a chocolate dessert, the golden liquid flowing in smooth ribbons, coating the surface and pooling at the edges, macro detail showing tiny air bubbles and glossy reflection, warm studio lighting"

## Image Prompt Templates

### Portrait / People

**Editorial Portrait**
"Editorial portrait of a woman in her 30s with natural curly hair, wearing a charcoal linen blazer against a blurred urban backdrop, shot at 85mm f/1.4 with soft window light from the left creating gentle catchlights, muted warm tones with shallow depth of field"

**Character Concept**
"Full-body character concept art of a cyberpunk street vendor, wearing a weathered leather jacket covered in holographic patches, neon-blue circuit tattoos visible on their neck, standing in a rain-slicked alley with steam vents, painted in a detailed digital art style with strong rim lighting"

### Landscape / Architecture

**Architectural Photography**
"Interior of a minimalist concrete art gallery, double-height ceiling with a single rectangular skylight casting a blade of warm light across polished concrete floor, a single large-scale painting on the far wall, shot at 24mm with leading lines drawing the eye inward, muted gray and warm amber"

**Fantasy Landscape**
"Towering Gothic castle carved into a sea cliff face, waves crashing against dark volcanic rock below, twilight sky with scattered cirrus clouds catching the last amber light, ravens circling the highest tower, painted in the style of detailed fantasy concept art, deep blues and warm amber highlights"

### Product

**E-commerce Product Shot**
"Pair of white minimalist sneakers on a clean light gray background, shot from 3/4 angle showing sole detail and stitching, crisp studio lighting with soft shadows, commercial photography style, neutral tones with bright white product pop"

**Flat Lay**
"Overhead flat lay of a curated workspace: leather-bound notebook, brass pen, ceramic espresso cup, succulent plant, and vintage brass compass arranged on weathered oak desk surface, soft diffused natural light from above, warm earth tones"

### Logo / Design

**Modern Logo**
"Minimalist geometric logo design for a tech company, abstract interlocking shapes forming a stylized 'N', clean lines on white background, gradient from deep navy to electric blue, scalable vector aesthetic"

**App Icon**
"Rounded square app icon design, abstract sound wave visualization in gradient teal and purple, dark background, subtle glass reflection overlay, iOS design language, clean and recognizable at small sizes"

### Illustration / Art

**Fantasy Art**
"Digital painting of a solitary astronaut standing on the surface of an alien world, a massive ringed planet dominating the sky above, bioluminescent flora glowing in soft greens and blues across the rocky terrain, painted with visible brushstrokes in the style of classic sci-fi illustration"

**Sci-Fi Concept**
"Interior of a derelict space station corridor, emergency lighting casting red-orange pools on the floor, overgrown with alien vegetation breaking through wall panels, a single shaft of white light from a hull breach, detailed concept art style with strong atmosphere and narrative tension"

## Negative Prompt Templates

"blurry, low quality, deformed, distorted, extra limbs, extra fingers, disfigured, bad anatomy, text, watermark, signature, cropped, out of frame, worst quality, jpeg artifacts"

### For Illustration / Art
"photographic, hyperrealistic, 3D render, stock photo, generic, clipart, low effort, blurry, text, watermark"

### For Video (General)
"blurry, low quality, distorted faces, text overlay, watermark, static image, frozen frame, glitch artifacts, unnatural motion, jittery, flickering"

### For Product Shots
"busy background, harsh shadows, unwanted reflections, text, hands, people, cluttered, low resolution, color cast"
